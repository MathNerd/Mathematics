$[a,b]$ into $n$ closed-intervals of equal length: First let’s define a function $\mathfrak{partition\_point}:\{0,..,n\}\to\mathbb{R}$ as follows: **(definition 1):** $\forall $\mathfrak{partition\_point}(0)=a$ and $\mathfrak{partition\_point}(n)=b$ **(fact 2):** $\forall i\in\{0,...,n\},\mathfrak{partition\_point}(i)\in [a,b]$ **(fact 3):** $\forall i\in\{0,...,n-1\},\mathfrak{partition\_point}(i)\lt \mathfrak{partition\_point}(i+1)$ **(fact 4):** $\forall i,j\in\{0,...,n\},i\lt j\longrightarrow \mathfrak{partition\_point}(i)\lt \mathfrak{partition\_point}(j)$ **(fact 5):** $\forall i\in\{0,...,n-1\},\mathfrak{partition\_point}(i+1)-\mathfrak{partition\_point}(i)=\frac{b-a}{n}$ **(fact 6):**
