\mathfrak{partition\_point}(j)$ **(fact 5):** $\forall i\in\{0,...,n-1\},\mathfrak{partition\_point}(i+1)-\mathfrak{partition\_point}(i)=\frac{b-a}{n}$ **(fact 6):**
